FAQ

How quickly can I get started?

Our AI receptionist can be deployed on your website within minutes, no coding required.

Can I customize the AI's responses?

Yes, all plans include customization options. Max and above tiers offer advanced AI training capabilities.

What about data security?

We maintain the highest security standards with end-to-end encryption for all messages and payment processing.

What about overages?

Additional messages are billed at $0.08/message for Starter, $0.05/message for Pro, $0.03/message for Max, and custom for Enterprise.
